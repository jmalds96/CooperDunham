Practice Areas

Patents

One of the services we perform for our clients is preparing and prosecuting patent applications through issuance in the United States Patent and Trademark Office. Our patent prosecution practice includes reissues, reexaminations, appeals and interferences before the United States Patent and Trademark Office Board of Patent Appeals and Interferences and Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit, foreign patent prosecution and opposition proceedings.

We also represent both patent owners as well as persons accused of infringement in patent infringement lawsuits and proceedings in the Federal District Courts and the International Trade Commission in all technology areas.

We also perform a variety of patentability, validity, infringement and right to use studies and opinions for both Fortune 100 corporations as well as start-ups. Given our size and technical expertise, we can generally perform these studies more quickly and efficiently than our competitors.

Unfair Competition

In our unfair competition practice, we represent clients in issues relating to unfair trade practices, including Federal unfair competition actions under Section 43(a) of the Lanham Act and state law causes of action, such as the right of publicity, and violations of noncompete and nondisclosure agreements.

Computer Software/Internet Protection

Our computer software/internet protection practice includes advising our clients on computer software intellectual property issues and internet/new media issues, including web site development and use, clearing of web pages, assisting with registration and policing the internet for violations of client's property rights, enforcing trademark rights against cybersquatters and other infringers, initiating and defending arbitration complaints under applicable policies of the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN), and advising on the use and protection of trademarks on the internet.

Copyrights

Our copyright practice includes evaluating works for copyrightability, including registrability, fair use and scope; preparing and filing copyright applications with the United States Copyright Office for all types of works (e.g. software, works of art, labels, fabrics, web site pages, audio visual media); and representing copyright owners and accused infringers in infringement litigation, including ex parte seizure orders, temporary restraining orders, preliminary injunctions and civil contempt proceedings in the Federal District Courts.

Licenses & Agreements

Negotiating and preparing licenses and agreements relating to all aspects of intellectual property rights, including patent applications, patents, trademarks, copyrights, know how and trade secrets.

False Advertising

As part of false advertising practice, we evaluate advertisements in all media for false advertising and claim substantiation, and representing clients in disputes, proceedings and litigation in the Federal Trade Commission, Federal District Courts and state courts.

Trade Secrets

Our trade secret practice includes advising our clients on procedures to maintain confidentiality of valuable technical and business information to achieve and maintain protectability as trade secrets, including preparation and negotiation of non-disclosure and confidentiality agreements, and representing trade secret owners and accused infringers in trade secret misappropriation disputes and litigation.

Trademarks

Our trademark services include evaluating prospective trademarks for clearance and availability for use, and for registrability in the United States Patent and Trademark Office. We undertake ex parte proceedings, such as filing and prosecuting trademark applications, and inter partes opposition and cancellation proceedings before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board; as well as policing the market for infringement and representing trademark owners and accused infringers in infringement litigation. Our trademark litigation practice includes ex parte seizure orders, temporary restraining orders, preliminary injunctions and civil contempt proceedings in the Federal District Courts and state courts.

Industries We Serve

For over a century, corporations doing business in all areas of the economy and of all sizes have entrusted their intellectual property matters to Cooper & Dunham LLP. Among the major corporations which the firm has represented in recent years include:

  • Pharmaceutical companies
  • Cellular handset manufacturers and carriers
  • GPS technology companies
  • Medical imaging and medical equipment manufacturers
  • Biotechnology companies
  • Pulp and paper manufacturers
  • Computer software, hardware and semiconductor companies
  • Entertainment and music products companies
  • Major department and specialty store chains
  • Famous jewelry and handbag designers
  • Major telemarketing companies
  • Makers of industrial machinery
  • Hedge funds and banks
  • Book and magazine publishers

The firm represents the interests in the United Stated of many foreign-based clients, including well known manufacturers of consumer electronics, cellular handsets, video graphics systems, photocopiers and cameras, X-ray equipment, and cookware, an aluminum refiner and pharmaceuticals.

Cooper & Dunham LLP also has a significant involvement in high-tech research conducted by major universities, hospital research centers and state-supported institutions.

The firm's clients span the range from the largest to the smallest. The firm advises numerous small manufacturing, marketing, financial and service businesses, as well as independent inventors, venture capitalists, entrepreneurs, authors, musicians and designers.

From their modern offices in midtown New York City, Cooper & Dunham's lawyers perform legal services for their clients throughout the nation and around the world. Through its network of foreign associates located in every country, the firm handles a full spectrum of intellectual property matters.
